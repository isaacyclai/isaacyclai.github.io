## Running
I enjoy long distance running in my free time, and my favorite distance is the half-marathon. My half-marathon PB is 1:28:04, which I ran in the Leiden Marathon 2025.

If you're interested in training to get faster, I recommend reading Jack Daniels' *Daniels' Running Formula* and Pete Pfitzinger's *Faster Road Racing* and *Advanced Marathoning* to learn about the different components that go into structuring training plans.

## Speedcubing
I used to be very involved in the competitive speedcubing community. My favorite and best event was the Megaminx, a 12-sided Rubik's Cube-like puzzle which I can solve [in 30 seconds](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PEBAtsZkLHM). In my prime, I was ranked 9th in the world and placed similarly at the World Championships. I've also won a national championship title and set some national records in the process.
